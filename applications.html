Applications

Where daylight is needed the most

Commercial buildings

Offices and commercial spaces often rely on artificial lighting throughout the day, especially in deep-plan layouts.

Daylight delivery can reduce daytime energy usage and improve occupant comfort.

Industrial and warehouse facilities

Large roof areas and long operating hours make industrial buildings ideal candidates for sunlight-based lighting systems.

Educational institutions

Natural light plays a key role in learning environments. Bringing daylight into classrooms improves visual quality without increasing energy demand.

Deep-plan and window-limited spaces

Basements, corridors, and interior zones often lack access to windows. Optical daylighting allows sunlight to reach these areas.
