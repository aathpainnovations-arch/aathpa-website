Sunlight and LED

Different roles, complementary systems

Artificial lighting

LEDs are efficient, reliable, and essential for nighttime and controlled lighting conditions.

However, they still consume electricity during daylight hours when natural light is freely available outside.

Natural sunlight

Sunlight offers full-spectrum illumination and changes naturally throughout the day.

Using sunlight directly reduces daytime electrical load and reconnects indoor spaces with the outdoor environment.

A hybrid approach

Aathpa’s system is designed to work alongside LED lighting. Sunlight handles daytime illumination, while LEDs support evenings and low-light conditions.

The result is a balanced, efficient, and resilient lighting strategy.
