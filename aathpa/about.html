About Aathpa

Rethinking how light enters buildings

Our starting point

Modern buildings depend heavily on artificial lighting, even during daylight hours. While efficient LEDs have improved energy usage, they still replace something fundamental—natural sunlight.

Aathpa was formed to explore a simple question: Can we use the sun itself as a primary daytime light source?

Our approach

Instead of converting sunlight into electricity and back into light, we focus on guiding sunlight directly into buildings using optical fiber.

The aim is not to replace artificial lighting entirely, but to reduce its daytime dependence while improving indoor light quality.

Current stage

Aathpa Lighting Solutions is currently in the prototype and testing phase. We are refining system design, performance, and integration methods.

We believe early collaboration with architects and builders leads to better solutions.
